Princess Mononoke

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki

Watch the Trailer!

Summary

Princess Mononoke is a Japanese animated epic historical fantasy film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki, produced by Studio Ghibli and released by Toho. The movie is set in the late Muromachi period of Japan with fantasy elements and tells the story of Ashitaka, a young prince who becomes involved in a struggle between the gods of a forest and the humans who consume its resources.

Characters

  • San (Princess Mononoke)
  • Ashitaka
  • Moro
  • Jigo
