Suzume

Directed by Makoto Shinkai

Watch the Trailer!

Summary

Suzume is a Japanese drama film directed by Makoto Shinkai, featuring music by Haru Yamada. The movie follows the story of a young woman named Suzume, who meets a man named Souta who talks about doors. Suzume follows him to a worn-away door and opens it to find a new world.

Characters

  • Suzume
  • Souta
  • Daijin
  • Tomoya
  • Tsubame
