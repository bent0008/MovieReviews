Howl's Moving Castle

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki

Watch the Trailer!

Summary

Howl's Moving Castle is a Japanese animated fantasy film produced by Studio Ghibli, directed by Hayao Miyazaki and based on the novel of the same name by Diana Wynne Jones. The movie follows the story of a young girl named Sophie Hatter, who is turned into an old woman by a witch and seeks the help of the wizard Howl, who lives in a magical moving castle.

Characters

  • Howl
  • Sophie Hatter
  • Calcifer
  • Markl
  • Witch of the Waste
