Your Name

Directed by Makoto Shinkai

Watch the Trailer!

Summary

Your Name is a Japanese animated romantic fantasy film produced by CoMix Wave Films, written and directed by Makoto Shinkai, and featuring character designs by Masayoshi Tanaka and music by Radwimps. The movie tells the story of a high school girl in rural Japan and a high school boy in Tokyo who suddenly and inexplicably begin to swap bodies.

Characters

  • Taki Tachibana
  • Mitsuha Miyamizu
  • Yotsuha Miyamizu
  • Yukari Yukino
  • Sayaka Natori
